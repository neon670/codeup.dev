Seafreight Forwarding:

  • Farasoobar Int’l serves the global shipping market as an ocean transportation intermediary.
  • Our own bill of lading gives us the ability to easily fulfill our customers requirements in terms of cost, transit time and service to any destination in the world.
  • Farasoobar ,since its inception has been serving import and export trade of any destination in the world.
  • We are able to route your good worldwide. With sea freight solution via service contracts with all major shipping lines operating in this region.
  • For LCL Farasoobar provides consolidation and groupage service with least re handeling.
  • From single shipments to multiple shipments to project cargo, our expert staff will arrange logical movements.

Airfreight Forwarding:

  • Air freight is taken care of by Farasoobar with great expertise by specialist staff with vast experience of service in the air freight industry.
  • Professional staff is available round the clock to handle air shipments.
