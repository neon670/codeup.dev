What is LEEDS

The leadership in energy and environmental Design (LEED) Green building Rating System (TM) encourages and accelerated global adoption of sustainable green building and development practices through the creation and implementation of universally understood and accepted tools and performance criteria.

Leading the industry in the movement for sustainable building is the United States Green Building Council (USGBC). Formed in 1993, it is compromised of over eight thousand members including federal, state, and local goverment agencies, collages, environmental groups, manufacturers, trade associations, architects, engineers and builders. Through their combined efforts these agencies are trying to affect a shift in the construction paradigm.

Having completed many LEED projects and with all areas of construction shifting towards "green building standards" GCC has and will continue to educate its employees on all LEED building standards.
