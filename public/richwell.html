About RGF

Richwell Global Forwarding has achieved a strong strategic position in the logistics industry with the Group’s own fleet of prime movers and special handling equipment, gaining a sturdy foothold in global forwarding, local inland freight and ground handling.


Guided by a belief that logistics would be a part of every business, a group of entrepreneurs gathered resources and devoted their passion to actualize their dreams – Richwell Global Forwarding.


Dedicated in integrating the forwarding and supply chain business, Richwell adopts the concept of providing a one-stop service solution to meet the evolving challenges of future logistics. A strong believer of service, Richwell equips itself with professional logistics software together with experienced and service oriented staff to optimize work processes and logistics balance sheet of every business.


Through extensive agency coverage coupled with Singapore’s excellent geographical location, Richwell assists organizations in making strategic decisions, expanding businesses globally. Striving to position itself as a global’s local forwarder, Richwell Global Forwarding offers a full spectrum of logistics services to ensure competitiveness in day-to-day operations of tomorrow’s business.


Richwell Global Forwarding, forwarding excellence....

  • Long term support from business associates & customers.
  • Listening & understanding customers’ every requirement & feedback.

RGF Sky Excellence

With Richwell’s Sky Excellence as your capable logistics partner, we empower you with flexibility, coupled with economies of scale. You, dictate the pace, aligned with price. Our excellent air transport connections carries you effortlessly across the skies, even to the most remote areas of the world. RGF Road Express

RGF Road Express has an extensive network, linking up all major cities domestically or globally. With our own fleet of trucks together with professional operators and advanced monitoring systems, we assure you maximum flexibility and reliability.


Our Group assets:

-50 units of Prime Movers
-193 units of 20’ Trailers
-237 units of 40’ Trailers
-12 units of 45’ Trailers
-13 units of 40’ Platform Trailers
-8 units of Low Bed Trailers
-2 units of 150 tons Heavy Duty Tractors
-2 units of Canvas Covered Containers for local & loose cargo transportation
-Container storage facilities at various locations with Kalmar services for container lift on/ off
-Container yards at Jalan Buroh & Penjuru Lane

RGF Project Swift

Customization of complex tasks, our Project Swift team presents creativity and skills, offering integrated solutions for heavy or oversized cargoes.
Regardless of distance, we are able to bridge continents with in-depth knowledge, precise planning and unique solutions, catering solutions for every individual requirements.

Our specialty covers:

1.On-site surveys by experienced Project Logistics Team

2.Customization of shipping concepts

3.Providing the appropriate equipments for heavy lift & oversize cargoes

4.Invigilating ground handling & ensuring cargo safety

5.Careful selection of quality carriers

6.Compliance with the guidelines of each exporting country

7.Vigilant monitoring of shipments till arrival

RGF ChemiSpec

 

In Richwell, ChemiSpec promises you that the handling & transportation of your Dangerous Goods are in safe hands. Our professional DG safety advisors adopt prudent handling of your cargoes in accordance to the prevailing DG transportation regulations. The safety & reputation of your business is our priority. RichStore

Streamlining warehousing activities at optimal costs, RichStore’s logistics professionals assure clients of quantifiable benefits through our intelligent warehouse solutions.

Strategically located facilities harnessed with excellent space management, RichStore promises you of favorable proximity to order fulfillment & distribution. The systems & technology of our contract warehouses provide proper cargo handling & accurate inventory tracking. With a decrease of investments in your fixed assets, appropriate transportation & value added services, we provide cost effective solutions, giving you a competitive edge.

 

RGF Global Porter

RGF Global Porter, a specialized hand-carry service, provides a competitive edge with maximum flexibility at maximum effectiveness. Combining high speed pick-ups, transfer to airport, hand over to on-board courier and completing the transaction by delivering the shipment with a signed proof of delivery, cargoes get to destinations as fast as a couple of hours.

What our Porters carry:

1.Documents (Including original contractual agreements, designs and drawings blueprint)

2.Parcels

3.Automobile parts

4.Pharmaceutical supplies

5.High value items

6.Computer components

What our Porters do not carry:

1.Drugs

2.Weapons or strategic goods

3.High value items

4.Jewels or gold

5.Live animals
