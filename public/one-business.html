X2 Logistics Networks X2 Group was formed with a vision of providing freight forwarders with a professional networking option. Since then X2 Group has quickly expanded not inly its own logistics networking options but has also expanded into the sectors of media and network management consulting. X2 Logistics Networks was X2 Group's first venture and has its beginnings when Richard Overton formed the company with the purpose of bringing professionalism to the logistics networking industry. Richard recognized the gap that existed within the freight forwarding industry. Since then X2 Group and X2 Logistics Networks have sought to establish relationships with companies that are seeking more professional dealings with global partners. Today X2 Group has 613 corporate customers in 162 countries around the globe. Through X2 Logistics Networks, X2 Global Media and One Monocle, the X2 Group has expanded our service offerings to allow us to best support our clients in competitive and drastically changing market.
